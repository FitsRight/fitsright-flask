Input your measurements

We offer two convenient methods for inputting your measurements:

  1. Manual: If you don’t have access to one of our 3D scanners, you can manually measure yourself. Despite this, you still gain access to all of our size recommendation services.
  2. 3D Body Scan: Upon receiving your 3D Body Scan, you’ll find it accessible in both your avatar and profile sections within the app. This method stands as the most precise way to generate your size recommendations.
